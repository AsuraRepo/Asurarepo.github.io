About AnnaPurna 🐘

South Indian Vegetarian Meal Planner · v4.0

🍽️ What is AnnaPurna?

AnnaPurna is a complete South Indian vegetarian meal planner designed to help families plan authentic traditional meals with all the right accompaniments — kuzhambu, rasam, poriyal, kootu, papad, pickle and curd together as a proper rice lunch should be.

Named after the Hindu goddess of food and nourishment 🌱, AnnaPurna respects deep South Indian traditions — especially Thenkalai Iyengar cuisine — while embracing 9 regional cooking traditions.

✨ Features

🍳 Full breakfast sets with sambar, 2 chutneys & podi
🍡 7-component rice lunches — traditional spread
🣒 Kids menu — 79 healthy fun recipes
🎉 Festival calendar — 84 dates, special menus
🗺️ 9 cuisines — Iyengar, Tamil, Kerala, Andhra & more
📅 1 week to 3 months of meal plans
🛒 Auto grocery lists — category sorted
🖨️ Print poster — A4 landscape, B&W
